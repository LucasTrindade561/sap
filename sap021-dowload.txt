report zr00026.

* tabelas transparentes
tables zt001.

* tabela interna
data: t_zt001 type table of zt001 with header line.

* tela de selecao

  perform f_seleciona_dados.
  perform f_download.

  select * from zt001 into table t_zt001.
endform.
